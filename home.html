About the Resort

Stellenbosch Golf Course Resort is one of the most sort after places to visit in South Africa. It is situated in Stellenbosch town in the Western Cape province just about 50km from central Cape Town. Being established in 1679, It is the second oldest town after Cape Town in the province. It is also commonly referred to as the 'City of Oaks' or 'Eikestad' in Afrikaans due to the fact that a large number of oak trees were planted by its founder, Simon van der Stel. Stellenbosch is home to one of the top universities in Africa (Stellenbosch University), and some of the best wine farms.
This 4 star hotel and resort is ideal for a weekend getaway from the busy city of Cape Town to just relax with nature in the secluded area of Stellenbosch. With its various range of accommodation options ranging from various types of hotel rooms to self-catering units that are affordable, it is ideal there is something suitable for every one of our guest!
